How It Works

Territory is computed using Voronoi nearest-player distance: each point on the map belongs to whichever team's closest alive player is nearer.

Blue = CT territory
Red = T territory

If T pushes forward, the area behind them remains T-controlled (since no CT player is closer). Boundaries shift as players move and die.
